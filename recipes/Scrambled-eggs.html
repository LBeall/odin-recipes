Scrambled Eggs

Description

Simple and fluffy scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/8 cup milk
  • 3 Tbsp. cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place oil in pan on medium heat.
  2. Crack eggs into pan.
  3. Add milk to the eggs.
  4. Cut the mixture together to scramble.
  5. Keep moving eggs in pan until they're full cooked.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
