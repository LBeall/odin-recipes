Peanuttbutter Fudge

Description

Soft and sweet home made Peanut Butter Fudge.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 Tbsp. peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a medium sized saucepan add ingredients and cook over medium heat.Stir until smooth.
  2. Once the mixture begins to rapidly boil, stir the mixture constantly for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir an additional 2 minutes.
  4. It will begin to dull slightly, pour into a pie pan and leave to cool.
  5. Cool completely, slice and serve.
